Q1. What command is used to insert multiple documents in MongoDB?

The command insertMany() is used to insert multiple documents at once in a collection.

Q2. What does the $or operator do?

The $or operator retrieves documents that satisfy at least one of the given conditions.

Q3. How do you sort documents in MongoDB?

You use the sort() method.

1 means ascending order

-1 means descending order
Example: .sort({ Price: -1, Title: 1 })

Q4. What is the use of $set in an update query?

The $set operator is used to modify the value of a field without affecting other fields in the document.

Q5. What does skip() do in MongoDB?

The skip() method is used to skip a specified number of documents in the output.
It‚Äôs commonly used for pagination when viewing data in parts.

Q6. What is the difference between updateOne() and updateMany()?

updateOne() updates only the first document that matches the condition.

updateMany() updates all documents that match the condition.
